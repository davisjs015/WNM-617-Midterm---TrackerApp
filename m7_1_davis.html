Jasper

Jasper

Age: 19 years old

General Information

Jasper is the adult male at Reserve Du Dja. He is the partner of Kesi (please see her page). Jasper and Kesi recently had their first baby November 2016. Jasper is the dominant male in the zoo's horde.

Since Jasper is the dominant Adult male he takes on characteristics other males do not. The dominant adult male mandrills are very distinct in their appearance. They have a bright red stripe down their nose that is flanged by blue on either side and have a red tip. Most people know a mandrill male because their rump is a beautiful color. As the male matures their rump becomes shades of blue, purple, mauve and red. Dominant male's are also distinguishable because they are "fatter" than subordinate males.

J.J.

J.J.

Age: 7 years old

General Information

J.J. is a adolescent male at Reserve Du Dja. He is the sister of Kesi (please see her page). J.J. and Kesi were transferred to Reserve Du Dja after there father passed on. There sister, Tami and mother are at the Minkebe National Park. J.J. will be the subordinate male in the horde.

Adolescent male mandrills are similar to the femlaes in their appearance. They have a red stripe down their nose that is flanged by blue on either side with a red tip but the red is muted in comparison to the dominant males. The subordinate males also do not have the signature beautiful colored rump that the dominant males have.

© 2017 Jessica Davis

Kesi

Kesi

Age: 14 years old

General Information

Kesi is the sister of J.J., the partner of Jasper and the mother of Ajani. Kesi along with J.J., her sister Tami and mother were brought to Minkebe National Park when their father passed on.

Females have the same characteristics as subordinate males. They have a muted red stripe down their nose and on the tip of the nost flanged by the blue sides. They have a blue and purple colored rump which is clearly not as bright as the dominant males. Females breed every two years and lasts for three months, June to October. With a gestation typically lasting 175 days.

Ajani

Ajani

Age: Born November 28, 2016

General Information

Ajani is the offspring of Jasper and Kesi. Newphew of J.J. He was born at Reserve Du Dja.

Mandrills are typical parented by just their mother. There is some alloparenting observed in Mandrill hordes but it occurs with the birthing mother's relatives helping care for the young. Ajani will continue sticking with his mother until he is six years old. This is when Mandrills leave their natal group and live within the others in the horde.
